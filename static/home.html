Multi Take-Profit &
Trailing Stop Strategies

Maximize profits and protect gains with sophisticated exit management.

Take-Profit Levels

  • TP1 — First target, close partial position (e.g. 25%)
  • TP2 — Second target, scale out more (e.g. 25%)
  • TP3 — Third target, further reduction (e.g. 25%)
  • TP4 — Final target, close remaining position
  • Configurable distance % and close % per level

Trailing Stop Modes

  • Moving Trailing — Classic moving stop-loss following price
  • Breakeven on TP1 — Move SL to entry when TP1 is hit
  • Step Trailing — SL moves to TP(n-1) when TP(n) is reached
  • Profit % Trailing — Activate trailing after X% profit
  • Static SL — Traditional fixed stop-loss (no trailing)
